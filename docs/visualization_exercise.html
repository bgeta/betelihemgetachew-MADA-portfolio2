Visualization Exercise

This visualization excercise was conducted to replicate the line chart published on fivethirtyeight.com in the year 2016. The chart is call “On duty police officer deaths in the U.S. By Cause”. The data source for this chart was from the Officer Down Memorial Page(ODMP). In trying to access the website for this particular dataset, I was only able to get annual fallen officer statistics rather than an aggregate data beginning in 1700’s as depicted on the fivethirtyeightwebiste. Alternativly, I was able to look through statista U.S. law enforcement deaths 1786-2022, by cause | Statista which also used ODMP data, and found an aggregate dataset from the 1700’s to 2020. The only difference was that the data in statista focused on four major catagories and also added COVID as a new category. The below figure show teh chart I will attempt to replicate in this exercise.
